### Failed attempt...

I always record the trailers with 32 chunks of render distance. But over the last weeks, a mod has gone viral, called [Voxy](https://modrinth.com/mod/voxy). Its permise is pretty simple: have 2048 chunks of render distance, without compromising FPS, thanks to the power of level of details (aka LODs).  
Importing this map into Voxy took litteral hours (and ate 128GB of storage in the process). But once the chunks were rendered, the performance hit was unnoticeable.  
However, Flashback, yet supported by Voxy seemed to keep some kind of hole between the LODs and the actual chunks. I will retry later on, hopefully when the mod will be less unstable.

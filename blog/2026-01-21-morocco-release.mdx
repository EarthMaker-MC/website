### Morocco, first African map for EarthMaker !

Yes, our first African map ! Morocco is a well-known (really?) country that is quite big by the way. And yes, usually, generating African countries take even more time than for European ones, as they are much bigger.
But here we did with Morocco !!
As usual, just join our [Discord server](https://download.earthmaker.world) to download the map!
